About the day

What is Umembeso?

Umembeso is the ceremony where the groom's family formally presents gifts to the bride's family, a joyful step that honours both families and blesses the union to come. Expect vibrant colour, music, dancing, and a warm welcome for everyone who joins us.

Gift Presentation

The groom's family presents blankets and gifts to the bride's family, witnessed by both households.

Song & Dance

Traditional song and dance carry the celebration, with both families singing as they welcome one another.

Feasting Together

A shared meal follows, bringing both families and all our guests together at one table.
